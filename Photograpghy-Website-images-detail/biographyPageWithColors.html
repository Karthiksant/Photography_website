BIOGRAPHY

Karthik is a Hyderabad based photographer who specialises in black and white photography. His work is often abstract and incorporates elements of surrealism, geometry, high contrast and the realities and diversities of human life.

    An insane photographer with his camera phone. In the realm of street photography, he freezes the rhythm of daily life, capturing genuine emotions in the faces of people. With a keen eye,he also explores nature places, transforming landscapes into visual moments.With his camera phone and a touch of Adobe Photoshop.

    And a fusion of urban stories and the nature to the power of simplicity in storytelling. His lens narrates, edits refine, and every click is a chapter in his visual journey.
